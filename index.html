Known for his curiosity and innovative mindset, he is passionate about technology and creating secure solutions that make a difference. Our love story

First time we meet

IIt all started when I replied to Srushti's story one day. She had seen me around college before, but we hadn’t really interacted. After my reply, we began talking more and got to know each other better. During my engineering exams, when I was buried in studies, we even started video calling, and that’s when we really connected.
One day, after my exam paper, I was heading towards the Ganesh temple, and she happened to be standing there with her friends. As I passed by on my bike, she called out to me, saying, "Nehal, I saw you! How much attitude do you have?" That moment stuck with me, and I knew I had to meet her in person. So, I decided to meet up with her. I went with my friends to DSB Café, which is located on our college campus, and that’s where we had our first official meet. It was the beginning of something special, and I’m so glad we took that step.

First date

Our first date was unforgettable. We decided to ride to Tiger Point on my bike, and it turned out to be the perfect choice. As we reached the top, we were greeted by a breathtaking sunset, painting the sky in beautiful shades of orange and pink. Sitting together, taking in the view, it felt like time stood still. There was something magical about sharing that moment with her, just the two of us, feeling the cool breeze and enjoying the serene beauty of nature. The very next day, we went to Makabawa Café, a cozy spot that became special for us. It was there that she surprised me with a thoughtful gift. I never expected it, and it made the moment even more memorable. Her kindness and attention to detail left me speechless, and I knew then that this was the beginning of something truly amazing.

Proposal

On December 16, 2023, the proposal was one of the most unforgettable moments of my life. It happened when she was staying in Ghatkopar, Mumbai, for her internship. Even though we were apart, we stayed connected, and I could feel the excitement building up.
It all started when I caught her in a moment of honesty. During our conversations, there were a few instances where she had been lying to me about certain things, and I caught her. Instead of feeling upset, I gently confronted her, and we talked through it.
But then, during that conversation, things took an unexpected turn. In a moment of surrender, she opened up completely, telling me how much she valued me and how important I was to her. She apologized for the lies and shared how she couldn’t imagine her life without me. And then, out of nowhere, she proposed to me.
I was completely taken aback and, to be honest, speechless. Her proposal, coming right after that honest moment, left me in awe. It was a mix of surprise, joy, and disbelief. I never saw it coming, but in that instant, I knew I wanted to spend my life with her. It was truly one of the most unexpected and beautiful moments of my life.

I want to Say something

As we approach our first anniversary, I can’t help but feel an overwhelming sense of love and gratitude for you, Srushti. On December 16, 2025, we will celebrate an entire year of love, growth, and unforgettable moments. In this year, we’ve shared laughter and joy, but also faced challenges and disagreements. And through it all, we’ve learned how to resolve, how to understand, and how to grow together. Every fight, every difficult moment, has brought us closer and strengthened our commitment to each other. What I feel for you is beyond words. It's a love that deepens every day, and the thought of spending my life with you fills my heart with so much happiness. You’ve become my rock, my support, my love. I can’t imagine a future without you by my side. Here’s to one year of us, and to many more years of love, laughter, and endless memories. I love you more than ever, and I can’t wait to continue this beautiful journey together, hand in hand.
